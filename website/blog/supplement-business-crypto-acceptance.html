Why Supplement Businesses Are Switching to Cryptocurrency Payments

By VirdisPay Team October 17, 2024 6 min read

The Supplement Payment Problem

Dietary supplement and wellness product businesses face unique payment processing challenges. Traditional processors classify them as "high-risk" due to FDA regulations, leading to 5-7% fees, rolling reserves, and frequent account terminations.

Why Traditional Processors Avoid Supplement Businesses

  • FDA scrutiny: Processors fear regulatory liability
  • Health claims: Concerns about marketing compliance
  • Chargebacks: Higher dispute rates than average merchants
  • Subscription models: Recurring billing increases risk profile

The Cryptocurrency Advantage

1. Dramatically Lower Fees

Traditional processing: 5-7% + monthly fees
Crypto payments: 0.5% + $0.01 network fee

Example savings on $50,000 monthly sales:

  • Traditional (6%): $3,000/month
  • Crypto (0.5%): $250/month
  • Savings: $2,750/month ($33,000/year)

2. Zero Chargebacks

Crypto transactions are final. This eliminates:

  • Chargeback fees ($25-$100 each)
  • Product loss from fraudulent disputes
  • Risk of account termination due to chargeback ratio

3. No Banking Restrictions

Crypto operates outside traditional banking:

  • No merchant account needed
  • No compliance reviews of product claims
  • No sudden account freezes
  • Complete payment processing independence

Stablecoins: Crypto Without Volatility

The key to accepting crypto without price risk is using stablecoins like USDC:

  • Always worth exactly $1.00
  • Backed by US dollar reserves
  • No volatility - perfect for business transactions
  • Can be converted to USD anytime
