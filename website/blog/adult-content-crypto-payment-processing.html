Adult Content Payment Processing: Why Cryptocurrency is the Solution

By VirdisPay Team October 17, 2024 8 min read

The Adult Industry Payment Crisis

Adult content businesses face systematic discrimination from traditional payment processors. Despite being a legal industry generating billions annually, banks and payment providers regularly:

  • Refuse to open merchant accounts
  • Charge 8-15% transaction fees (vs. 2-3% for mainstream businesses)
  • Impose 20-30% rolling reserves
  • Terminate accounts without warning
  • Block entire business categories

Why Payment Processors Discriminate

Reputational Risk

Banks fear association with adult content damages their brand image, despite the industry's legality and size.

High Chargeback Rates

Adult businesses experience elevated chargebacks due to:

  • Purchase denial ("I didn't buy that")
  • Subscription disputes
  • Spousal chargebacks
  • Friendly fraud

Card Network Pressure

Visa and Mastercard impose strict rules and can ban entire merchant categories, leaving businesses without recourse.

The Exorbitant Cost of Traditional Processing

Typical fees for adult content businesses:

  • Transaction fees: 8-15%
  • Rolling reserves: 20-30% held for 180+ days
  • Monthly gateway fees: $100-$500
  • Chargeback fees: $50-$100 each
  • Compliance/monitoring fees: $200-$500/month

Example: $100,000 monthly revenue

  • Processing fees (10%): $10,000
  • Reserve held (25%): $25,000
  • Monthly fees: $400
  • Total cost: $10,400 + $25,000 locked up
