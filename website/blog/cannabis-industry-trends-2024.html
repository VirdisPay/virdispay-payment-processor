Cannabis Industry Trends 2024: The Rise of Crypto Payments

By VirdisPay Team January 22, 2024 9 min read

The cannabis industry is experiencing unprecedented growth and transformation in 2024. With the global cannabis market projected to reach $102.2 billion by 2030, businesses are rapidly adopting innovative solutions to overcome traditional barriers and capitalize on emerging opportunities.

One of the most significant trends reshaping the industry is the widespread adoption of cryptocurrency payments. As traditional banking continues to pose challenges for cannabis businesses, crypto payments are emerging as the preferred solution for forward-thinking companies looking to scale efficiently and cost-effectively.

Market Growth and Projections

Global Cannabis Market Size

The cannabis industry continues its explosive growth trajectory:

  • 2024 Market Size: $57.7 billion globally
  • 2030 Projection: $102.2 billion (77% growth)
  • US Market Share: 42% of global market ($24.2 billion)
  • Hemp Market: $8.5 billion globally in 2024

Regional Growth Patterns

  • North America: Leading market with 65% of global cannabis sales
  • Europe: Fastest-growing region with 89% annual growth rate
  • Asia-Pacific: Emerging market with significant CBD growth
  • Latin America: Increasing medical cannabis adoption
"The cannabis industry is experiencing a perfect storm of factors driving growth: expanding legalization, increasing consumer acceptance, innovative product development, and technological solutions like crypto payments that solve fundamental business challenges."

  • Customer Preference: 78% of cannabis customers prefer crypto payments
  • International Expansion: 340% increase in cross-border cannabis commerce

2. Smart Payment Routing Technology

Advanced routing systems are optimizing cannabis transactions:

  • Network Optimization: Automatic selection of most cost-effective blockchain networks

Regulatory frameworks are becoming more cannabis-friendly:

Cannabis consumption patterns are evolving:

  • Age Distribution: 35-54 age group now represents 42% of cannabis consumers
  • Gender Balance: 51% of new cannabis consumers are female
  • Income Levels: 67% of cannabis consumers earn over $50,000 annually
  • Education: 73% of cannabis consumers have some college education

Purchase Preferences

  • Online Shopping: 78% of cannabis consumers prefer online purchasing
  • Payment Methods: 84% prefer digital payment options
  • Privacy Concerns: 91% value transaction privacy
  • Convenience: 89% prioritize fast, easy checkout processes

Artificial Intelligence Applications

International Market Expansion

Global Legalization Progress

Cannabis legalization is expanding worldwide:

  • Europe: Germany, Luxembourg, and Malta leading EU legalization
  • Asia: Thailand, South Korea, and Japan expanding medical cannabis
  • Latin America: Uruguay, Colombia, and Mexico with established markets

  • Institutional Investment: 67% increase in institutional cannabis investments

Green Cannabis Movement

Environmental consciousness is driving industry changes:

  • Carbon Footprint: 89% of cannabis companies implementing sustainability programs
  • Renewable Energy: 67% of cultivation facilities using renewable energy
