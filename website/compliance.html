  • Registration: We comply with FCA regulations for crypto asset businesses
  • Anti-Money Laundering: Full compliance with UK AML regulations
  • Customer Due Diligence: Enhanced due diligence for high-risk customers
  • Transaction Monitoring: Real-time monitoring for suspicious activities

  • Medicinal Cannabis: Compliance with MHRA regulations for medicinal cannabis businesses
