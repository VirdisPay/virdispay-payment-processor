## VirdisPay Launches Cryptocurrency Payment Processing Platform for Regulated Industries

### New Financial Technology Solution Addresses Payment Processing Challenges Facing Businesses Excluded from Traditional Banking

**[CITY, DATE]** - VirdisPay, a financial technology company, today announced the launch of its cryptocurrency-based payment processing platform. The solution is designed to address payment processing challenges faced by businesses in regulated industries that have difficulty accessing traditional banking services and payment processors.

The platform enables businesses to accept cryptocurrency payments with reduced transaction fees compared to traditional payment processors, while maintaining compliance with Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulations.

### Industry Challenges

Many businesses in regulated industries encounter significant obstacles when seeking payment processing solutions:
- Limited access to traditional payment processors and banking services
- Higher transaction fees compared to standard retail businesses
- Concerns about account stability and unexpected service terminations
- Complex compliance requirements that add operational overhead

### Platform Capabilities

VirdisPay's platform addresses these challenges through several key features:
- Cryptocurrency payment acceptance with reduced transaction costs
- Blockchain-based infrastructure that operates independently of traditional banking relationships
- Integrated compliance tools including KYC and AML verification
- Non-custodial architecture that provides merchants with direct control over their funds
- Automated network routing technology that optimizes transaction costs

### Transaction Fee Structure

The platform operates on a transparent fee structure starting at 0.02% per transaction, compared to industry-standard rates of 2.9% to 3.5% plus additional fees charged by traditional payment processors. This represents a substantial cost reduction for merchants processing significant transaction volumes.

### Compliance and Security

VirdisPay has built compliance and security features directly into the platform architecture:
- Automated KYC/AML verification processes
- Risk assessment and monitoring tools
- Document management systems
- Regulatory compliance frameworks
- Non-custodial design that ensures merchants maintain control of their funds

### Smart Payment Routing

The platform utilizes automated routing technology that selects optimal blockchain networks based on current transaction costs, supporting multiple stablecoin options including USDC, USDT, and DAI across networks such as Polygon, Binance Smart Chain, and Arbitrum.

### About VirdisPay

VirdisPay is a cryptocurrency payment processing platform designed for businesses in regulated industries. The company is headquartered in London and focuses on providing financial technology solutions for legal businesses that face challenges accessing traditional payment processing services.

The platform enables businesses to accept stablecoin payments with reduced transaction fees, automated network routing, and built-in compliance tools. VirdisPay operates as a non-custodial platform, ensuring merchants maintain direct control over their funds while benefiting from integrated compliance and security features.

### Availability

VirdisPay is now available for businesses operating in jurisdictions where cryptocurrency payments are legal. The platform offers multiple service tiers designed to accommodate businesses of various sizes, from small startups to enterprise-level operations.
