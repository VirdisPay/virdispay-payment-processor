### 2. **Prepare Your Launch**

**Best Day to Launch:** Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday
**Best Time:** 12:01 AM PST (California time)

**Why:**
- More people are active mid-week
- Better chance of getting featured
- More time to build momentum

### 3. **Build Your Network**

**Before Launch:**
- Create Product Hunt account
- Follow other products
- Comment on recent launches
- Build relationships with hunters

**Find a Hunter:**
- Product Hunt "Hunters" can feature your product
- Look for hunters in FinTech/crypto space
- Reach out 1-2 weeks before launch
- Offer to help with their products too

### 4. **Prepare Your Launch Day**

**Schedule:**
- **12:01 AM PST:** Launch goes live

- **Throughout Day:** Engage with every comment
- **Evening:** Thank supporters, share results

**What to Prepare:**
- Quick responses to common questions
- Demo video ready
- Screenshots ready
- Personal story/why you built it
