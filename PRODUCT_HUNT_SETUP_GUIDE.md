## üöÄ Step 3: Submit Your Product

### **When to Submit:**

**Best Day:** Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday  
**Best Time:** 12:01 AM PST (California time)

**Why:**
- More users active mid-week
- Better chance of being featured
- More time to build momentum

### **How to Submit:**

1. Go to **Product Hunt** ‚Üí Click your profile
2. Click **"Submit a Product"**
3. Fill in the form:

   **Basic Info:**
   - **Name:** VirdisPay - Crypto Payment Processor for High-Risk Industries
   - **Tagline:** 99.98% lower fees for cannabis, CBD, and high-risk merchants
   - **Website:** https://virdispay.com
   - **Category:** Fintech / Payments / Cryptocurrency

   **Description:**
   - Paste the description from Step 2

   **Gallery:**
   - Upload 5-7 images
   - Add captions to each

   **Video (Optional):**
   - Add your demo video URL

   **Founder/Maker:**
   - Add yourself as the maker
   - Add your team members (if any)

4. Click **"Submit for Review"**
5. Product Hunt will review (usually 1-2 days)
6. Once approved, you'll get a launch date
